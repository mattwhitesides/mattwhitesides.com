1. If using a school computer ignore step 2 most school PC's (at least in the engeneering buildings) have Notepad++ already installed
  2. Download the software from the link above or here
  3. Install of course then open the program.
  4. In the top bar find the "Plugins" selection and go to "Show NppFTP Window" (Plugins -> Show NppFTP Window)
  5. A new side window menu thing should pop up.
  6. In that new toobar at the top right of it should be a "Gear icon" click it and chose "Profile Settings" (Gear Icon -> Profile Settings)
  7. A new window should pop up, at the bottom right of it click the "Add New" button to setup a new profile
  8. Enter whatever name you want for your profile (ex: babbage or Mizzou or whatever)
  9. In the "Username" Field enter "babbage.cs.missouri.edu" (Username: babbage.cs.missouri.edu)
  10. For the "connection type" chose "SFTP" (Connection Type: SFTP)
  11. This should automaticly set the "Port" to "22", if it dosen't make it "22" (Port: 22)
  12. In the "username" field enter your "pawprint" (Username: "yourPawprint")
  13. In the "password" field enter your "password" (Username: "yourPassword")
  14. Now you can click close leaving all default settings unchanged, no saving or anything is needed it will automaticly save
  15. Now click the plug icon thing at the top left of the SFTP side bar window, then click your profile name
  16. If everything is correct you should see your babbage directories and you can fully use them like local files, saving will auto upload to babbage.
  17. You can also use this interface to upload, download, change names, delete files, etc from the icons in the window on your babbage account
